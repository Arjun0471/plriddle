How to Play

Guess the Premier League player in 8 tries.

  • Green: Exact match.
  • Yellow: Close (e.g., goals/assists within 2, age/apps within 5).
  • Gray: No match.
  • Arrows show if the mystery player has more or less.
  • Use the silhouette for a hint!
